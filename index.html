Biography

Xian-Zhang Chen (陈宪章) got his Ph.D. in ICPCS at Lanzhou University (2016.09-2023.06), and his doctoral supervisor is Prof. Liang Huang. During this period, he also worked as a joint Ph.D. student (2020.12-2022.11) hosted by Prof. Rodolfo Jalabert in IPCMS, University of Strasbourg, France. Now he work with Prof. Tong Zhou as a PostDoc in EITech, Ningbo, China.
